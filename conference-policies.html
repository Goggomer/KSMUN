Conference Policies

Delegate Code of Conduct

Generally, all delegates should display good behaviour and refrain from doing anything that may be considered violent or disrespectful. Any and all of the following acts will result in consequential action:

  • Theft
  • Found in possession or under the use of drugs/alcohol
  • Violence
  • Unnecessarily missing committee sessions (after reminders and warnings)
  • Academic Dishonesty, which includes plagiarism as well as the use of AI tools to generate works

Comedy and jokes are allowed provided that they are not hurtful to any person or group. Comedy is welcome as long as it does not negatively interfere with otherwise important procedures. All delegates are required to attend all committee sessions without reasonable reason and without informing either your committee staff members or the secretariat team.

Technology Use Policy

During committee sessions, delegates are authorized to use electronic devices and the Internet for educational purposes only. Non-academic use of electronic devices and internet access (e.g., texting, gaming, etc.) is strictly prohibited at all times. Earbuds and other headphones are also not permitted unless pre-approved by the dais team or the Secretariat. According to the KSMUN Delegate Code of Conduct, staff personnel may request that delegates who violate this policy cease their disruptive and unproductive conduct, and they may suspend their technology access.

Frequently Asked Questions:

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a high school simulation of the United Nations. Delegates who attend the conference are assigned to represent a nation or person in a committee addressing global issues. The variety of committees allows delegates to debate a wide range of topics, from security to more imaginative scenarios involving magic. This experience helps delegates become more aware of the UN's functions and enables them to articulate their country’s stance on global events. Additionally, MUN teaches valuable skills, such as negotiating with others. Overall, Model UN provides an opportunity for delegates to investigate and debate pressing issues, fostering a deeper understanding of international relations and diplomacy.

How can I register?

All delegates attending KSMUN 2024 are required to register through their school’s delegation. Once your school has registered, individual delegates from that school will be able to register. You must complete the registration form and select the committee and position you wish to represent (though this does not guarantee you will be assigned to your selected position). Following the completion of the form, you will be notified within two weeks of your committee assignment.

What is KSMUN?

KSMUN stands for Khalsa School Model United Nations. This is the first iteration of the conference, providing delegates with a truly enriching experience. KSMUN takes pride in ensuring that everyone leaves with new experiences and information.

Where and when will KSMUN 2024 take place?

KSMUN 2024 will be taking place on December 7, 2024. This is a one-day conference. It will be held at Khalsa Secondary School which is located at 10589 124 St, Surrey, B.C. Unfortunately, due to there being no restaurants within walking distance from the location lunch will be provided. Delegates will be provided with a food restriction form before the conference.

How much does it cost to attend KSMUN?

The cost to attend KSMUN is $25.00.

When is registration available?

Registration will be available as soon as possible!

What committees will delegates have to choose from?

Currently there are 3 committees to choose from: UNSC, NATO and FCC

How can I get involved with KSMUN?

As of now KSMUN is not opening application for the Secretariat or staff team.

Does KSMUN have double delegation?

As of this year all delegates will be representing their country/person individually.

Is there a limit to size of delegation?

There will be no limit to size of delegations.

Can I win awards?

Yes, awards are presented to delegates who go above and beyond in representing their country or person. Those who receive awards have worked diligently to earn them, distinguishing themselves from others through their exceptional performance.

Do you provide financial aid or scholarships?

Unfortunately, KSMUN will not be providing scholarships or financial aid.

Are teacher chaperones mandatory?

Teacher chaperones are mandatory for school delegations attending KSMUN 2024. The chaperones will be responsible for their delegates throughout the conference.
